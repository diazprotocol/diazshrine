  • Diaz loves gifts and compliments—regardless of how patently you lie to her.

  • Her longest streaming session has been Stray (10h 24m).
  • Diaz has been two times to Japan.
  • Yellow and orange are among Diaz's least favourite colors.
  • Diaz's favorite food is spaghetti with tomato sauce and feta cheese.
  • Diaz doesn't really know where the continents are.
